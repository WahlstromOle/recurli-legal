Common Topics

  • Notifications not appearing: Check notification permissions in your device settings and make sure notifications are enabled for Recurli.
  • Subscription issues: Use "Restore Purchases" in the app if your Pro access does not appear after reinstalling or changing devices.
  • Account deletion: In the app, go to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account.
  • Data sync questions: Ensure you have an active internet connection so reminders and instances can sync correctly.
