Recurli ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Recurli (the "App").

2. Automatically Collected Information

Device Information

  • Device type and model

Usage Information

  • Feature usage statistics

Subscription Information

We use the collected information for:

1. Providing Core Functionality

  • Creating and managing reminders
  • Sending push notifications
  • Syncing data across sessions
  • Anonymous user authentication

2. Subscription Management

  • Processing payments
  • Managing subscription status
  • Handling refunds and customer support

3. App Improvement

  • Analyzing app usage to improve features
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Understanding user preferences

4. Legal Compliance

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Protecting our rights and preventing fraud
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

Data Location

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by:

  • Supabase (database hosting)
  • Servers located in accordance with our service providers' policies

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is deleted immediately when you delete your account through the app
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export your reminder data

Correction

  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Modify your account details within the App

Deletion

  • Delete your account directly from the app (Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account)
  • All data is deleted immediately upon confirmation
  • Delete individual reminders at any time
  • Important Notes:
    • Users with active monthly/yearly subscriptions must cancel first
    • Lifetime Pro users can delete their account but will permanently lose their purchase
    • Lifetime purchases cannot be restored to a new account or refunded

Opt-Out

  • Disable push notifications in device settings
  • Opt out of analytics (where applicable)

To delete your account, go to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account in the app. For other requests, contact us at recurli.app@gmail.com.
